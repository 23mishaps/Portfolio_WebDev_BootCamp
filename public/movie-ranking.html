The Best Movies According to Misha

My Top 3 Movies of all Time

Elemental

This movie is so beautiful. The colors compliment eachother perfectly, and the story line is captivatingly emotional. There were a few times though out the movie that I wanted to cry, but in a good way.

Spirited Away

Spiried away is one of my all time favorite movies. I first watched Spirted Away when I was just a little girl, and no matter how many times I watch it, it never gets old. It is a timeless movies.

In This Corner of the World

In This Corner of the World is a slice of life animated film that takes place in Japan during WWII. The story follows a young girl as she enters an arranged marriage and adapts to her new family and their way of life. The end is unbareably sad, but the movie in total is a great watch.
